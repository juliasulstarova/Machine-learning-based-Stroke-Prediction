Hello and Welcome to the Stroke Prediction Project!


What is in this directory:

1- Demo: a python tkinter GUI with our random forest trained model embedded. You can fill the demo infromation, check your stroke risk adn our prevention advice.

2- Preprocessing and Training files: we have trained over 15 models in our project. This direcory can show you all of the preprocessing done and the training and evaluations done in one click. 

How to get started? 

For the demo: 
go to the demo directory, open your terminal then execute: 

python stroke_demo.py

for the training: 
go to the training directory, open your terminal then execute: 

python train_stroke_data.py
